My personal favorite drinks:

The menu can be super over whelming having an idea of a few drinks you might want to try before you walk in can ease this feeling.

Everyone has different taste, but theres something for everyone. Heres some of my personal favorites

Cold Refreshers Drinks (all have caffeine)

~ The Pink Drink, a strawberry coconut cold refresher

~ Kiwi Starfruit Lemonade, A sour kiwi refresher drink

~ Black Tea Lemonade, this is like a classic arnold palmer drink, I add peach juice for a little extra flavor
Frappuccinos (all have caffeine)

~ Vanilla Bean frappuccino, a vanilla blended shake with whipped creme. This drink taste like a vanilla milkshake except it has caffeine.

~ Strawberries and Cream Frappuccino, a strawberry blended shake with whipped cream. This drink is very much like the Vannila drink except its strawberry.

~ Double Chocolaty Chip Frappuccino, a chocolate blended shake with whipped cream. As you guessed this drink taste like a chocolate milkshake.

Hot Drinks

~ Peach Tranquility, this is a hot minty tea perfect for sickness.

~ Blonde Roast, a light coffee perfect for coffee lovers who want a simple coffee.
